About S & I Car Rentals

Our Story

Founded in 2025, S & I Car RENTALS has been providing exceptional car rental services to customers across the country. With a fleet of over 1000 vehicles and locations in major cities, we make car rental simple, affordable, and convenient.

Our mission is to provide reliable transportation solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers, whether they're traveling for business, leisure, or any other purpose.
